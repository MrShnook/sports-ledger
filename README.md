# SportsLedger - Youth Sports Expense Tracker

## Overview

SportsLedger is a mobile-first web application designed to help parents of youth athletes manage and track the ever-increasing costs associated with competitive sports. 

The core value proposition is to provide a simple, automated way to answer the question: "How much are we *really* spending on sports?"

## Problem Statement

The cost of youth sports in the U.S. is a multi-billion dollar industry, yet parents lack dedicated tools to manage these expenses. Costs are fragmented across league fees, travel, equipment, private training, and more. This financial chaos makes it difficult to budget, plan, and understand the total investment in their child's athletic career. Reddit research confirms parents are actively seeking a solution to this problem.

## Key Insights & Validation

- **Strong Market Need:** Parents on platforms like Reddit are explicitly asking for a tool to track these specific, fragmented expenses.
- **Large Market Size:** The estimated Total Addressable Market (TAM) is $1.2B, with a Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM) of $138M.
- **High Margin Potential:** As a SaaS product, it has the potential for 80%+ profit margins.
- **Competitive Landscape:** The space is relatively open. While generic budget apps exist, none are tailored to the unique categories and cadence of youth sports expenses. Some early competitor signals (domain registrations) were detected in late 2025, indicating a first-mover advantage is still in play.
